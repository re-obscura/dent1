Cosmetic Dentistry

Whitening, bonding, and porcelain veneers to create a natural, confident smile — tailored to your individual goals.

Transform Your Smile with Confidence

At Fairfax Dental Office, our cosmetic dentistry services are designed to help you achieve the smile you've always wanted. Whether you're looking for subtle enhancements or a complete smile makeover, our experienced team uses the latest techniques and technology to deliver natural, beautiful results.

Our Cosmetic Services

Professional Teeth Whitening

Our in-office whitening treatments can brighten your smile by several shades in just one visit. We also offer custom take-home kits for your convenience. Our whitening solutions are safe, effective, and tailored to minimize sensitivity.

Porcelain Veneers

Porcelain veneers are thin, custom-made shells designed to cover the front surface of your teeth. They can correct chips, gaps, stains, and minor misalignment — giving you a flawless, natural-looking smile that lasts for years.

Dental Bonding

Dental bonding is a quick, cost-effective way to repair minor imperfections. Using tooth-colored composite resin, we can fix chips, cracks, gaps, and discoloration — often in a single appointment.

Free Cosmetic Consultation

Not sure which treatment is right for you? Schedule a free cosmetic consultation and let us help you explore your options with no pressure and no obligation.

Inlays and Onlays

For teeth that need more than a filling but less than a crown, porcelain inlays and onlays provide a durable, natural-looking restoration. They are custom-fabricated in our office using CEREC same-day technology.

Why Choose Fairfax Dental Office for Cosmetic Dentistry?

  • Advanced CEREC same-day technology for crowns and restorations
  • iTero digital scanning — no messy impressions
  • Personalized treatment plans based on your goals
  • Experienced cosmetic dentists with an eye for detail
  • Comfortable, modern office near Courthouse Metro
  • Flexible scheduling including Saturdays
